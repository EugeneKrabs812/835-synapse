DOPAMINE

Reward. Motivation. Anticipation.

What is Dopamine?

Dopamine is neurotransmitter involved in reward processing, motivation, learning and goal-directed behavior. It does not simply create pleasure - it drives anticipation.

Mesolimbic Pathway

Dopamine neurons from the ventral tegmental area (VTA) project to the nucleus accumbens. This circuit plays a major role in reinforcement learning and habit information;

Dopamine & Adolescence

During adolescence, dopamine sensitivity increases. This contributes to heightened reward-seeking, risk-taking, and strong social motivation.
