The Teenage Brain

Why do we overthink?

During adolescence, the prefrontal cortex is still developing, while the amygdala is highly reactive. this imbalance can amplify emotional processing and self-reflection.

Why is dopamine so powerful?

Teenagers show increased sensitivity to dopamine the neurotransmitter linked to reward and motivation. This affects risk-taking, social validation, and habit formation.

Why do we procrastinate?

Conflict between the limbic system and the prefrontal cortex can lead to short-term reward preference over long-term goals.
